Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 · Smart Coating Ltd, Malta

1. Who we are

This website is operated by Smart Coating Ltd, a company registered in Malta ("we", "us", "our"). Contact: sales@smartcoating.it

2. What data we collect

When you purchase access to our products, we collect:

  • Your email address (required for delivery of your access code)
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Stripe. We never store your card details.
  • Your IP address and browser type (collected automatically by our hosting provider, Netlify)

We do not require account registration. We do not collect your name unless you voluntarily provide it during checkout.

3. How we use your data

  • To send your access code by email immediately after purchase
  • To provide customer support if you contact us
  • To comply with legal obligations (tax records, etc.)

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for marketing without your explicit consent.

4. Third-party services

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy applies to all payment data: stripe.com/privacy
  • Netlify — website hosting. Netlify may collect server logs: netlify.com/privacy
  • Supabase — access code database hosted in EU (Stockholm). Your email is not stored in Supabase — only the access code and its status.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the site to function. Stripe uses cookies for fraud prevention during checkout. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

6. Data retention

Email addresses are retained for as long as necessary to provide support and comply with tax obligations (typically 7 years per Maltese law). You may request deletion at any time by contacting us at sales@smartcoating.it.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to: access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion, object to processing, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact: sales@smartcoating.it

8. Data transfers

Your data is processed within the EU/EEA where possible. Stripe and Resend may process data in the United States under appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses).

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.
